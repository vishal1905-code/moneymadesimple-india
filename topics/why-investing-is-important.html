What is Investing?

Meaning

Investing means using your money in a smart way so that it grows over time. Instead of keeping money idle, investing allows it to work for you.

How Investing Works

When you invest, your money is used by businesses or governments to create value. If they perform well, your money grows.

Why Investing Matters

Saving alone is not enough because inflation reduces the value of money. Investing helps beat inflation.

Important Things to Know

  • All investments involve risk
  • Long-term investing works best
  • Never invest emergency money
